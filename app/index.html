What is an NFT?

NFTs are unique digital items such as collectibles, artwork, or game items. Each NFT is unique and exists in a single copy, it cannot be divided, and all information about its author, buyer and all transactions with it is securely stored in the blockchain system. In other words, an NFT is a digital certificate attached to a unique object.
